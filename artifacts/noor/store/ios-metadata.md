## Description (4000 chars max)
Anssam (أنسام — "Gentle Breezes") is a premium, privacy-first Islamic companion designed for Muslims worldwide.

✦ PRAYER TIMES
Accurate salah times calculated locally using the adhan algorithm — no server required. Supports all major calculation methods (MWL, ISNA, Egypt, Makkah, Karachi, Gulf, Kuwait, Qatar, Singapore, France, Turkey) and both Shafi and Hanafi madhabs. A live countdown shows time remaining until the next prayer.

✦ HOLY QURAN
All 114 Surahs with full Arabic text, transliteration, and translations in English, French, and Turkish (via the trusted AlQuran.cloud API). Beautiful reading experience with bismillah and verse-by-verse display.

✦ QIBLA DIRECTION
Precise Qibla bearing calculated from your location toward Makkah al-Mukarramah.

✦ TASBIH COUNTER
Digital dhikr counter for Subḥān Allāh, Al-ḥamdu lillāh, Allāhu Akbar, and more — with haptic feedback and visual progress ring. Tracks completed sets.

✦ 99 NAMES OF ALLAH
All 99 Asmāʾ ul-Ḥusnā with Arabic, transliteration, and meaning in your language.

✦ DUAS COLLECTION
Curated authentic duas for morning, evening, sleep, waking, eating, travel, mosque, and general occasions — with Arabic, transliteration, translation, and hadith source.

✦ HIJRI CALENDAR
Beautiful display of the current Hijri date alongside the Gregorian calendar.

✦ MULTI-LANGUAGE
Full interface and Quran translations in English, العربية, Français, and Türkçe.

✦ PRIVACY FIRST
No account required. No ads. No analytics. No data leaves your device. Location is used only locally to calculate prayer times and is never transmitted.

✦ PREMIUM DESIGN
Luxury dark emerald and gold theme designed for daily spiritual use.
